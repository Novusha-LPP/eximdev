# Privacy Policy for Suraj Forwarders Attendance

**Effective Date: May 13, 2026**

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to the **Suraj Forwarders Attendance** application. This Privacy Policy explains how **Novusha LPP / Suraj Forwarders** ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application and related services (the "Attendance Module").

By using the app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 2. Information Collection and Use

### 2.1 Location Data
To provide accurate attendance tracking and geofencing capabilities, the App requires access to your device's location services.
*   **Purpose:** We collect precise location data (GPS coordinates) specifically during the **"Punch In"** and **"Punch Out"** actions. This is used to verify that the employee is physically present at the authorized workplace location as defined by the organization.
*   **Disclosure:** The app collects location data to enable attendance verification at the time of recording your work sessions. We do not track your location continuously in the background unless explicitly required by your organization's policy and disclosed separately.

### 2.2 Personal Information
We may collect and store certain personally identifiable information to identify and manage your employee profile, including:
*   Name
*   Employee ID / Username
*   Organization / Department details

### 2.3 Device Information
We may collect information about the device you use to access the App, including:
*   Device model
*   Operating system and version
*   Unique device identifiers

## 3. Data Storage and Sharing
*   **Employer Access:** The data collected (attendance times and location of punches) is shared exclusively with your employer's administrative team for payroll and attendance management purposes.
*   **No Third-Party Sale:** We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information or location data to outside parties for marketing or advertising purposes.
*   **Secure Storage:** All data is stored on secure servers with industry-standard encryption protocols.

## 4. Permissions
To function correctly, the App will request the following permissions:
*   **Location (Precise/GPS):** Required to verify your location during punch-in/out.
*   **Internet Access:** Required to sync your attendance data with the central server.

## 5. Security
The security of your data is important to us. We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you enter, submit, or access your information.

## 6. User Rights
You can manage your location permissions through your device settings at any time. However, disabling location services may prevent you from successfully recording your attendance if geofencing is enabled by your organization.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date."
